Fun Facts About Pandas

  • Panda have great camouflage for their environment
  • Panda eyes are different from normal bears
  • Panda spend a lot of their day eating

Fun Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons can navigate via the earth’s magnetic fields
  • Pigeons have been known to live over 30 years
  • Pigeons are also known as the ‘Rats of the Sky’

Fun Facts About Camels

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms
